# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want to foster an open, welcoming, and professionally constructive environment in this project — regardless of experience level, professional background (strategy, technology, research, private use), or personal background.

## Expected Behaviour

- Respectful, substantive communication in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Constructive criticism of content, not of individuals.
- Care with content about real individuals — no knowingly false or misleading claims about the public record of the named directors.
- No publication of real personal data, confidential business information, or proprietary internal details.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of a named individual's publicly known positions.
- Publication of confidential or personal data of third parties.
- Use of the repository to generate content that violates the archetype disclaimer (see `CLAUDE.md` §7).

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported via the repository's issue tracker or directly to the maintainers. Maintainers decide on a case-by-case basis on appropriate measures (warning, restriction of contributions, exclusion from the project).
